About Nova D

More Than a Service - It Is a Movement

Nova D Consulting Group was built for the person who feels stuck, embarrassed, denied, or ready to give up on their financial future. The mission is to combine education, strategy, and compassionate accountability so clients can rebuild with confidence.

We understand that bad credit does not mean your future is over. Your score is not low by accident, and you do not have to stay stuck. NDCG helps you reset your profile, rebuild your confidence, and position yourself for better approvals.

  • Relatable, results-focused support
  • Customized dispute and score optimization strategies
  • Guidance for housing, auto, home, funding, and credit card approvals
  • Financial education that feels clear, modern, and empowering
